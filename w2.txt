You have a preliminary meeting to discuss some of the features for an existing frontend 
application, one of the senior members of the team suggests a solution that you strongly 
believe will not work. What, if any, actions would you take?



Answer:
I will listen care fully and try to understand proposed solution.
I will ask different questions which will be in my mind, might be senior have some extra 
knowledge and back ground of suggestion which I dont understand at first point.
I will discuss pros and cons of proposed solution with example with the team.
Like I will say I am worried that this proposed solution have some problem and will provide some examples.
I will also propose exploring alternative options, potentially through prototyping.
Through this collaborative process, we can reach a consensus on the best solution for the project
